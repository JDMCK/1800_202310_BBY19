Welcome to Fender Defender!

Our goal is to help B.C. drivers to decide whether or not the drive they are planning will be safe and to ease the stress of having to make the decision.

Who we are!

Fender Defender will not only give you the directions you need to go to your destined location, it will also give you recommendations on whether the route you want to take or if it is even safe based off the information the user provides to our database.

Safety first!

We are looking to take the ambiguity out of your safety, how? Simple! We take in your car's information: what type of vehicle you drive, the kind of tires you have on, the weather conditions and your overall comfort of driving in certain conditions. We put it all together, calculate the overall risk factor and provide a suggestion on how safe it is for you to get there.
